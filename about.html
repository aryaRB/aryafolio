Hi, I'm Alex

I’m a product designer with a passion for learning new things. I’m currently at Universe building tools to open up the possibilities of the internet to everyone.

Prior to Universe, I was a product designer at Block Renovation where I designed software to facilitate the future of home renovations.

Before Block Renovation, I was living in Seattle and attending the University of Washington. I graduated with a Bachelor of Design in Interaction Design in 2020.
‍
During the summer of 2019, I moved down to Mountain View, CA to work as a product design intern at Intuit where I worked with the QuickBooks Payments and Capital team.

Today, you can find me in Williamsburg trying to take a decent photo every now and then.
