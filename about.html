Hi there, I'm Arya

A Designer hailing from Dibrugarh, Assam who has a knack and passion for solving problems that people face with an instinct of learning new things. Currently working as a Product Designer at Shoopy Retail where I am building product for SMB owners to make their online presence easy.

Prior to Shoopy, I was a UI/UX intern at YEF and Coursera where I designed their app from scratch to production(only in YEF).

Before this, I was living in Kolkata pursuing Engineering from West Bengal University of Technology and graduated with a BTech Degree in Electronics in 2020.
‍
During my Engg days, phase of 2018-19, I was a part of the CoE team, joined as a design apprentice where I led a team of 4 in collaboration with BCC&I and Plumule reasearch on Business 4.0.
Apart from that I keep keen interest observing things, sketching, Gymming, Sprituality, Web Development and sometimes Photography.
